Materials Needed

  • Large Mixing Bowl
  • Small Mixing Bowl
  • Knife
  • Cutting Board
  • Lemon/Lime Squezer
  • Spoon
  • Fork

Steps

Step One

Use the lime squeezer to squeeze about 1/4 cup of lime juice. This prevents the guacomle and avocado from turning brown and keeps things fresh.

Step Two

Use a cutting board and knife to chop your onions and tomatoes. Then, thinly dice the cilantro and garlic so there are no big chunks of either ingredient.

Step Three

Combine the lime juice, cilantro, and onions into a bowl. Mix together with a teaspoon of salt.

Step Four

Peel your avocados and remove the pits using a sharp knife. Then, mash the avocado in a small bowl with a fork. after this, dice the tomatoes into small cubes.

Step Five

Combine all ingredients together into a large bowl.

Step Six

Add another sprinkle of salt over the top. Finally, serve with tortilla chips of your choice and enjoy!

Ingredient Amount
Avocado Three Whole Avocados
Lime One Lime
Tomatoe Two Whole Tomatoes
Onion One Onion
Cilantro One Tablespoon Chopped
Garlic Two Cloves
Salt A Few Pinches
Chips As Many As Needed
