Cookie Baking Tutorial

Recepie Name

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • i cup of butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). In a bowl, mix all dry ingredients together: flour, baking powder, baking soda. Then, put aside.
  2. Get a bigger bowl and combine the butter and sugar. Mix until smooth. Add the egg and vanilla and beat with a whisk.
  3. Grab about a teaspoon of dough and roll into small balls. Place them 2 inches apart on a buttered baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the oven for 8 to 10 minutes. Take out when golden brown on top.
    5.
  5. Let sit on a cool wire rack for 2 mintes, then Enjoy.
